<objective>
Port the store_core choke point (`_query`/`_write`/`_write_many`/bootstrap/readiness) from TuringDB to ArcadeDB — the single seam all nine mixins route through (ARC-04). Collapse the per-batch submit-before-match write model into one managed transaction with read-your-writes and commit-retry-N (D-08), delete the TuringDB CSV vector-load mechanism, and replace `load_graph`-style readiness with a real ArcadeDB probe wired to `/health` (D-10). Rewire `store_from_env` to construct an `ArcadeDBClient` from `ARCADEDB_*` env and wire those vars into the compose mcp service (ARC-02), retiring the FTS5-outbox bootstrap steps (ARC-06) — all add-only against the retained turingdb service/dependency.

Purpose: Nothing downstream (the mixin ports) can run until the seam speaks ArcadeDB. This is the foundation of Wave 4.
Output: A ported, tested store_core seam and production env wiring.
</objective>
