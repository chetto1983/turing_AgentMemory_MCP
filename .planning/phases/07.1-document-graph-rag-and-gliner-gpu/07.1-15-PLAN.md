over it and record the measurements.

baseline. **That verification is the whole value and is the property spike 003's 15 questions
lack.** The user reviews survivors for plausibility — the judgment Claude cannot make.
Output: the frozen question set, the three-arm driver, and raw measurements. The VERDICT is plan 16's.

    documents — plan 10's substrate makes this a direct traversal). Compose candidate two-hop chains
    per MuSiQue's recipe: hop-2's question must depend on hop-1's ANSWER, not merely mention a
    related entity. A question answerable by conjunction of two independently-retrievable facts is

    let the eval measure single-hop retrieval while calling it multi-hop.
