<objective>
Wire the flag-gated multi-hop document graph channel into the fusion port: seed entities from the
query, traverse `Chunk`<->`Entity` alternating hops, apply hop decay and D-03's hub damping, and
feed the result to `fuse_rankings` as `doc_graph`.

Purpose: D-12 resolves the wrinkle that "eval-first" cannot mean "no channel code" — the gate needs
a measurable channel. It means deciding what *grade* of code the gate reads: real, tested,
tenant-scoped, but inert unless enabled. The eval flips the flag to measure.
D-13: feed the EXISTING fusion layer. HippoRAG 2's PPR-with-dense-reset-mass is known-better and
**deliberately not taken** — it would invent a new ranking mechanism in a codebase whose entire
retrieval story is RRF, to chase a lift unproven for us. It is the recorded upgrade path if the
eval shows the channel matters and RRF is the limiter.
Output: a default-OFF document graph channel the eval can switch on.
</objective>
