<objective>
Close the second "missing" item of verifier gap 2: *"Capture and serialize real span/audit events in
leakage tests and assert no exact tenant identifier appears."*

The live harness already scans `caplog`, foreign/invalid error strings, view reprs, router status, and
manifests — but its `diagnostic_text` (built at `_arcadedb_physical_isolation_support.py:484-493`)
never includes span-recorder events, and the assembly store is wired with `NoopAuditSink()`
(line ~204). An `InMemorySpanRecorder` IS constructed (line ~205) but its `events` are never read.
That is precisely why the live gate stayed green while raw identity flowed into shared telemetry: the
leak was outside the asserted surface.

Purpose: a leakage test that cannot observe the leaking channel is the failure mode that let this gap
ship. This plan makes the harness observe the channel it claimed to cover, then closes the phase on
the full Definition-of-Done gate.
Output: a telemetry-aware live harness, live assertions, and a green full gate.

Scope: the harness's assertion surface and the closing gate. Do not extend live coverage to Phase 8
concerns (job cancel-recovery, upload session locking) or to the same-tenant read-then-create race —
all three are explicitly out of scope for this phase.
</objective>
