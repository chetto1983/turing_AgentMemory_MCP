<objective>
Close verifier gap 2 (BLOCKER): *"Logs, spans, audits, status, registry, manifests, errors, and
diagnostics expose opaque tenant correlation only, never raw `user_identifier` values"* — FAILED.

`_StoreCore._span` forwards attributes unchanged and `_StoreCore._audit` serializes the raw
identifier verbatim (`store_core.py:372-394`). The memory, document, and search mixins pass
`{"user_identifier": user_identifier, ...}` straight into the shared span recorder. The shared
observer and audit sink are process-wide across all tenants (`StoreSharedDependencies`), so every
tenant's exact identity lands in one shared telemetry stream — directly contradicting D-07 and the
documented Trust Boundaries contract.

Purpose: the phase's operational isolation contract claims pseudonymous diagnostics. Today the store
is the one component that breaks it, and the registry/manifest work of 05-01/05-02 is undermined by
telemetry that re-links the opaque database back to a real identity.
Output: central sanitization at `_span`/`_audit`, mixin attribute cleanup, a leakage test, and the
documented contract.

Scope: store telemetry. Registry, manifest, and router diagnostics were verified pseudonymous
already — do not rework them.
</objective>
