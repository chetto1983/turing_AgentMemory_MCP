<objective>
Build the deterministic gate engine (`scripts/gate_diff.py`) that: (1) verifies the gate
corpus against the committed sha256 manifest and fails closed on drift (D-11); (2) derives
the corrected TuringDB baseline e2e pass/fail from the committed capture's `detail` fields
without any live re-run (D-05); (3) computes the D-01 bug-corrected 7-doc bar, diffs the
port's retrieval metrics per-document AND aggregate within an epsilon band on the mean of
N runs, and emits a GO|NO_GO verdict (D-01/D-02/D-04). All logic is TDD'd against the
already-committed baseline JSON fixtures — no GPU, no live capture in this plan.

Purpose: This is the honest measurement core of the ARC-09 migration-correctness gate.
Every downstream artifact (gate-result.json, GATE.md) and the Phase-7 guard depend on this
engine producing a truthful, reproducible verdict.
Output: `scripts/gate_diff.py` (functions + CLI) and `tests/test_gate_diff.py`, all green.
</objective>
